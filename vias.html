Our Company Group

Emerge GLass India PVT. LTD (A group company of alstone) manufactures high quaity of Flat glass, Mirror and Frosted Glass. Powered by innovative capability and engineering expertise, the customize glass solution have created value and superior benchmarks. With glasses exported to Dubai, Srilanka, Brazil, Netherland, France, Nepal, Bangladesh and Turkey, the group takes immerse pride as the only entity 1mm thin flat glass in the country.

Recently Emerge Glass has ventured into the manufacturing of container glass in three catagories Liquor,Beer and Food Grade. The Company has imported state of the art plant and machinery from Bucher Emhart Glass, Europe with the capacity of 210 TPD.

The Alstone group has also established state of the art ACP plant in Dubai, UAE to manufacture Non-Combustable (A2) and fire Retardant (FR) Metal Composite Panel under brand name "Emerge UK". These products are tested in world's most reputed laboratory
